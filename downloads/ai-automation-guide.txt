AI Automation Guide

Overview
AI automation helps teams reduce manual work, improve accuracy, and scale operations.
This guide outlines practical use cases, a rollout plan, and governance basics.

Quick Wins (0-30 Days)
1) Identify repetitive tasks with clear inputs/outputs.
2) Start with internal workflows: lead routing, support triage, reporting.
3) Pilot one process, measure time saved, error reduction, and cost impact.

High-Impact Use Cases
1) Sales: lead scoring, outreach personalization, meeting summaries
2) Support: ticket classification, response drafting, knowledge search
3) Operations: invoice matching, approvals, anomaly alerts
4) Marketing: content briefs, campaign analysis, SEO insights

Implementation Checklist
1) Define objective and KPI (hours saved, SLA, accuracy)
2) Map process and data sources
3) Select model + tooling (API, workflow engine, RPA)
4) Build human-in-the-loop review for critical steps
5) Implement logging and monitoring
6) Train users and document the flow

Governance and Safety
1) Access controls and audit logs
2) Data minimization and PII handling
3) Clear escalation path for edge cases
4) Regular model performance review

Rollout Plan (60-90 Days)
1) Week 1-2: discovery and prioritization
2) Week 3-6: build pilot + QA
3) Week 7-8: launch to small group
4) Week 9-12: scale, optimize, and measure
